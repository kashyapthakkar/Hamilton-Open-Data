About

Explore Hamiltin helps you to get to know about many famous tourism places in Hamiltin city. The application is very user friendly. All you have to do is just search the place if you already know that where you want to visit or if you are totally new to the city and just randomly want to visit any place the select the place type from the navigation bar for example waterfall, shopping centers, etc. The map shows the places with pushpins assosiated with it(purple circle). The orange pushpin is your current location. Click on the pushpin of the any location you want to visit or get to familier with it will show one infobox in which you can find various functionalities like Toll-Free number of place, email link, website of place address, direction to the place etc. If you still have any question about how the applicationworks or want give any suggesions about application, feel free to mail us by clicking on mail icon on the right hand side of the screen.
(note: the email icon won't be found in mobile version, sorry for inconvenience)
